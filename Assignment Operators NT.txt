# Assignment Operators Demo
# Assignment Operators

x=10
print("Initial value:",x)

x+=5 #x=x+5
print("After+=:",x)

x-=3 #x=x-3
print("After-=:",x)

x*=2 #x=x*2
print("After*=:",x)

x/=4 #x=x/4
print("After/=:",x)

x//=2 #Floor division
print("After//=:",x)

x%=3 # Modulus
print("After%=:",x)

Output 
Initial value: 10
After+=: 15
After-=: 12
After*=: 24
After/=: 6.0
After//=: 3.0
After%=: 0.0
